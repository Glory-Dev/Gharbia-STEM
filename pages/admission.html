Admission

Being admitted to STEM Schools

The Admission Process

For a student to be eligible to enroll in a STEM School, they must be:

  • Of Egyptian Nationality
  • Of age no more than 18 years old
  • A current Grade 9 student

To be eligible for the admissions test, a Grade 9 student must satisfy one of the two cases:

Case: 1

The student’s total score in grade 9 is 98% or more (274.5 out of 280)

In this case, the student doesn’t need to have a full mark in any of the subjects

Case: 2

The student’s total score in grade 9 is 95% or more (266 out of 280)

In this case, the student MUST have a full mark in any of the (Maths, Science, English)

Mid to Late July, the applications to the admissions test open:

The Student applies Online through the admissions portal, where they enter their personal information, and rank the 21 STEM Schools based on personal preferences

  • an Official 9th Grade transcript
  • an Official Birth Certificate
  • a Number of personal images of size 6x4
  • a printout of the schools ranking printed from the application website
  • a Bank receipt of transfer of 150 L.E. to the Ministry of Education
  • a Number of copies of the students's parent National ID Card'

Test dates usually occur in Mid August:

Students head to their test locations announced on the website, and enter a digital test compromised of 4 sections:

MATH Section
60 Questions in 45 Minutes
ENGLISH Section
60 Questions in 45 Minutes
SCIENCE Section
60 Questions in 45 Minutes
IQ Section
60 Questions in 45 Minutes

Results are out in the first week of September

Students get their results from the same website they applied from.

Unfortunately, waitlists have been canceled since the 2021 admission cycle, students are either accepted or rejected.

Accepted students are informed of the STEM School they got accepted to and their overall ranking based on the total score of the admissions test.

Accepted students should head to the STEM School and provide the school with the following papers:

You can find all the papers needed to be provided here.
