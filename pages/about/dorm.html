Dormitory & Campus Life

The Dormitory acts as the student's home during the school year

The Dormitory Building

The main dormitory building is separated into 2 sections, the Boys' and the Girls' section, connected by the cafeteria, each section contains 4 floors apart from the ground floor, each floor contains a studying hall along with 10 residential rooms with a maximum capacity of 4 people per room, making for a total maximum capacity of 320 students.

Residential Rooms

Each room contains 4 separate beds, 4 drawers, and 4 cupboards, rooms are also provided with a ceiling fan, power outlets, and 2 ceiling lamps.

At the beginning of every school year, same-year students are assigned a random room they have to share with 3 other roommates.

Studying Halls

Each studying hall contains a number of chairs and tables that can hold a huge number of people, these halls serve as both quiet areas for focused study and a communication center where students can gather and study in groups, studying halls are provided with high-speed, free to access Wi-Fi to support the students' work.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria serves the 3 daily meals for the students, breakfast, lunch, and dinner, each meal contains balanced amounts of the nutrients the human body needs, the cafeteria operates in a fixed time schedule:

Breakfast: 7:00 AM - Lunch: 3:00 PM - Dinner: 8:00 PM

Leisure & Sports

The school campus offers many different ways to practice sports and hobbies, the campus' different sports fields; the soccer field and the basketball field can be accessed during the school day in breaks and in Physical Education (PE) sessions, the fields are also open to students after the school day until midnight, student athletes can also use the sports fields to practice for major sports events and state championships.
