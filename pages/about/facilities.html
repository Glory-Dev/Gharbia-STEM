School Facilities

The School Building

The main school building and facilities are a 3 minute walk from the dormitory building, the school offers a number of facilities for its students to do scientific experiments, and build physical prototypes, inside the school's 5 Science Labs and Fabrication Lab, the school facilities are accessible during the school day and the fabrication lab is open until 8:00 PM.

Classroom

Inside the classrooms, the student is the main focus, with the teacher being an instructor, students can freely discuss the learning materials with their classmates, students can also use the technology available in each classroom to enhance and speed up the learning process, the classrooms are organized to enforce students to communicate their ideas and practice problems together

Science Labs

The School offers a number of scientific laboratories where students can apply what they learned in the classrooms, the labs are prepared with sny tools and devices the students might need to perform any scientific experiment, the labs are also used to test the students' practical skills and dealing with lab equipment, the science labs are accessible during the school day, teachers are well trained to help the students' with any experiment.
