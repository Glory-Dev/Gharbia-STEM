Learning Outcomes (LOs)

Our curriculum's applied learning and project- based approach exposes students to real-life applications in STEM fields to provide them with the necessary tools to contribute to solving problems in their communities and excel as scholars, Students are also challenged inside the classroom through leading sessions, working on group projects, and engaging with the subject material.
