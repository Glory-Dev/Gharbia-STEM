Introduction

Located at the Gharbiya STEM School campus, the Gharbia Hack Club provides a way for high school students to know more about programming, and learn to build something useful using the resources the club provides, helping the students to gain knowledge and experience, the club is mainly focused on web development, and provides the needed resources for the knowledge hungry members. There are also some other departments such as competitive programming and app development.

The club additionally focuses on practical work, through assigning tasks and challenges for the members to push their capabilities to the max.

The Gharbia Hack Club values determination and dedication in its members, people who are willing to do their best in order to achieve a goal are welcomed in the club regardless of their background.

Teaching Plan

The way members of the Hack Club can gain knowledge and experience is through self-learning, the Club provides the various online free easy-to-access resources for the members, and they do the rest, assigned with a mentor that is responsible for assigning tasks and creating quizzes to test comprehension of programming topics, the mentor also grades the assignments and provides each member with individual constructive feedback, the mentor provides assistance to the members that need it and is the main factor of the learning process besides the members’ dedication towards learning and passion to computer science.

Objectives

  • Export professional programmers

    The club mainly aims to export peers with programming mindset, developing their critical thinking, and communication skills making them able to deal with any programming languages and contribute to projects they know nothing about in a couple of days through teaching them the concept of programming, practice problem-solving, and apply their knowledge in real-life projects

  • Fill the gap & Projects Presentation

    Provide students with the essential skills to build websites from scratch and help them present their work and projects in global competitions, research studies, school capstone project, etc…
