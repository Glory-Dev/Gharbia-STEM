Our Alumni

Our school is ranked first among all STEM Schools with reference to our students grades.

Most alumni end up studying a major related to engineering, medicine, or science fields in general in an Egyptian public university, with the majority of students getting into top rank universities, including Cairo University, Giza University, Menofia University, etc...

Notable Alumni

Students from our last four graduating classes (2019 - 2022) have enrolled in universities in Africa, North America, and Europe, pursuing their dream education and careers across the globe
