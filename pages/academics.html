Academics

The School offers a rigorous, STEM-based curriculum that sets students up for academic excellence at university

Empowering Students Through Applied Learning

Our curriculum's applied learning and project- based approach exposes students to real-life applications in STEM fields to provide them with the necessary tools to contribute to solving problems in their communities and excel as scholars, Students are also challenged inside the classroom through leading sessions, working on group projects, and engaging with the subject material.

Capstone Projects

Upon their graduation, every student will have finished five capstone projects addressing Egypt's technological, environmental, and public health challenges, The School nourishes the students to work in teams and connect what they learn from the curriculum with ideas to solve real-life challenges, enhancing their critical thinking, problem-solving, presentation, and writing skills.

Capstone Projects consist of different parts: Portfolio, Prototype, Poster, and Journals, Capstone projects account for 60% of the total score in Grades 10 and 11, in Grade 12, Capstone only accounts for 20% of the final score
